Yes! Most games are completely free. Some premium titles are available for one-time purchase.

How do I join a tournament?

Head to the Leaderboard page and look for active tournament registrations. A valid account is required.

How do I report a cheater?

Use the in-game report button or email support@nexusplay.gg with the player's username.

Yes, our mobile app is available on iOS and Android with full feature parity.
