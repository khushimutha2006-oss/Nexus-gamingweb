◆ MISSION

FORGED FOR COMPETITION

NexusPlay was born in 2020 from a garage in Seoul, by three gamers who were tired of lagging servers, pay-to-win mechanics, and hollow online experiences.

We set out to build the most honest, most competitive, most electrifying gaming platform on earth. Today, over 2.4 million players call NexusPlay home.

Our mission is simple: every player deserves a fair fight.
